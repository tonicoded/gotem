privacy policy.

How gotem handles photos, AI scans, HealthKit step rewards, battles, subscriptions, and support data.

last updated · july 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how gotem collects, uses, and shares information when you use the gotem mobile app and related services. gotem lets you scan real animals, generate collectible creatures, build a deck, earn rewards, and battle locally or online where available.

1. data we collect

Depending on how you use gotem, we may process:

  • Camera photos and scan images: photos you capture or select to detect an animal, verify that a scene is real, crop the subject, and create a collectible.
  • AI scan inputs and outputs: detected animal names, prompts, confidence signals, generated names, lore, moves, type, rarity context, and other AI-generated details.
  • Collection data: caught creatures, cards, stats, levels, rarity, IVs, shiny status, battle moves, catch history, balls, boxes, trainer XP, rank, and local game progress.
  • HealthKit step data: step count data you allow the app to read so gotem can award mystery boxes and show step progress.
  • Permission status: whether camera and HealthKit access are available so onboarding and scan flows can work correctly.
  • Multiplayer data: device identifier, player code, username, online status, battle invites, battle sessions, selected creatures, battle actions, and timestamps where online battles are enabled.
  • Subscription status: Apple transaction, entitlement, renewal, expiration, revocation, and restore status used to provide gotem Pro access.
  • On-device app data: onboarding status, settings, cached images, local diagnostics, and saved app preferences.
  • Support messages: information you choose to send when contacting support, including screenshots or details you include.
  • Android waitlist: the email address you voluntarily submit on the gotem website to receive Android launch updates.
  • Website analytics: anonymized page, referrer, approximate location, device, operating system, and browser information used to understand website traffic. Vercel Web Analytics does not use cookies.

2. how we use data

  • Detect animals from photos and verify that scans appear to come from real-world scenes.
  • Generate collectible creatures, cards, moves, stats, names, and descriptions.
  • Save your collection, trainer progress, inventory, boxes, battles, and app settings on your device.
  • Read HealthKit steps, with your permission, to award movement-based mystery boxes.
  • Run local battles and, where enabled, sync online battle invites and turn-based battle state.
  • Validate, restore, and enforce gotem Pro subscription access.
  • Prevent abuse, troubleshoot issues, maintain reliability, improve app behavior, and respond to support requests.
  • Send Android availability and launch updates when you join the Google Play waitlist.

3. who data is shared with

We do not sell personal data. We share limited data with service providers only as needed to operate gotem:

  • OpenAI: may receive scan photos, cropped image data, animal detection context, prompts, and generated output context needed for AI animal detection, real-scene checks, and collectible generation.
  • Supabase and hosting providers: may process Android waitlist email addresses, multiplayer player codes, device identifiers, online status, battle invites, battle sessions, and request metadata where online features or backend functions are used.
  • Apple: handles App Store distribution, StoreKit purchases, subscription billing, refunds, HealthKit permissions, camera permissions, and operating-system permission prompts.
  • Email and support providers: may process messages and attachments you send to support.
  • Vercel: hosts the website and processes anonymized Web Analytics data and request metadata.

4. healthkit and step data

gotem uses HealthKit step data only for in-app step progress and mystery box rewards. We do not sell HealthKit data, use it for advertising, or use it to make medical, employment, credit, insurance, or eligibility decisions.

HealthKit step data is processed primarily on your device. It is not sent to OpenAI for collectible generation. If you contact support, only include HealthKit or step details if you want us to see them.

5. AI animal analysis

gotem uses AI to detect animals in photos and generate fictional collectible creatures. AI output may be inaccurate, incomplete, inconsistent, delayed, or unexpected. AI results are for game and entertainment use and are not veterinary, wildlife, safety, medical, legal, or professional advice.

You are responsible for the photos you capture or submit. Do not scan private property, people, or animals in unsafe situations without permission.

6. local storage and retention

gotem stores most collection data, images, inventory, trainer progress, settings, and onboarding state on your device. Local data remains until you delete it in the app, reset the app, or uninstall gotem.

Data sent to OpenAI, Apple, Supabase, hosting providers, or support providers may be processed and retained according to their policies and the technical needs of the service. Multiplayer sessions and invites may be kept temporarily for synchronization, reliability, and abuse prevention.

Android waitlist email addresses are kept only as long as needed to send Android launch updates or until you ask us to remove your address.

7. permissions and controls

  • Camera: used to scan animals. You can change camera access in iOS Settings.
  • HealthKit: used to read steps for rewards. You can change Health access in iOS Settings or the Apple Health app.
  • Subscriptions: weekly and yearly gotem Pro subscriptions can be managed or canceled in Apple ID subscription settings.
  • Delete data: the app’s delete/reset option removes local collection, inventory, progress, images, and settings from your device.
  • Waitlist: you can request removal from the Android waitlist by emailing us at the address below.

8. no sale or third-party advertising

We do not sell personal data and do not use gotem data for third-party advertising.

9. security

We use standard safeguards including encrypted network transport. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

10. children

gotem is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

11. international transfers

Service providers may process data outside your country. This policy applies regardless of processing location.

12. changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.
